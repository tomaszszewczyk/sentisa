# Areas of application
 - augmented reality interactive floors
 - interactive boards
 - VR (for motion recognition)
 - camera based image recognition systems

# Specification
 - LED intensity control over USB
 - Camera HUB
 - Cooling fan control
 - Camera shutter control
 - Over-temperature protection
 - 24V DC power supply
